By: Alison Balbuena

How this works: there's no review-text data available in this dataset, so categories like "Study/Lock In" or "Coffee Chats" are inferred, not confirmed. Sweet Treats comes from matching bakery/dessert-style keywords in the cafe name. The others are estimated from review volume relative to nearby cafes (quieter → better for studying, moderate traffic + high rating → good for chats, high traffic → social hangout spot). Treat these as reasonable starting guesses, not guarantees — actual seating, wifi, and noise level aren't in this data.
